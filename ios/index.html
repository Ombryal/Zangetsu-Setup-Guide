Steps

  1. If a TestFlight link is available, join the beta directly from your iPhone.
  2. Otherwise, download the IPA from the link above and sideload it using AltStore or Sideloadly.
  3. Trust the developer certificate in Settings → General → Device Management.
  4. Open Zangetsu and complete any initial setup.
