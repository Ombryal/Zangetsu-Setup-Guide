Supported TV types

A single APK works on all supported TV platforms:

  • Android TV
  • Google TV
  • Fire TV
  • Other Android‑based TV devices

Steps

  1. Download the TV APK using the button above.
  2. Transfer the APK to your TV (via USB, cloud storage, or apps like Send Files to TV).
  3. Enable installs from unknown sources in your TV’s settings.
  4. Open the APK file on your TV and install Zangetsu.
  5. Launch Zangetsu and follow on‑screen setup.

System requirements

  • Android TV 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher
  • Fire TV OS 6+ or Google TV
  • At least 150 MB free storage
  • USB drive or network transfer method
